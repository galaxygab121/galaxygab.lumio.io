**Privacy Policy**  
Lumio collects only what is needed to run your child's routines and check-ins. We do not share or sell your data. Your child's information never leaves your family unless you choose to export it.

Lumio uses Supabase to securely store your data and RevenueCat to process in-app purchases. No personal data is shared with third parties for advertising purposes.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data by emailing galaxygab21@gmail.com. We will process it within 48 hours.
